Field polygon editor — 1.0×

Drag the numbered points onto the field corners/sidelines. Green 0–4 = near sideline (0 left → 4 right), orange 5–9 = far sideline (5 right → 9 left). The far line is the important one for far balls — give it a little margin *outside* the touchline if unsure. Scroll = zoom toward cursor, drag empty space = pan, drag a point = move it. Save when done.
